# TaxPilot AI Product Requirements

## Vision

Build a modern AI-powered web application that helps Indian taxpayers prepare and file their Income Tax Return accurately with minimal manual effort.

## Primary Users

- Salaried employees
- Investors
- Freelancers
- Small business owners

## Safety and Compliance Principles

- Never fabricate financial data.
- Always allow users to review and edit AI-extracted information before generating a return.
- Clearly distinguish imported data from AI-generated estimates.
- Keep tax rules versioned and easy to update.
- Clearly state that the app assists in tax preparation and that users are responsible for final verification.
- Recommend Chartered Accountant review for complex tax situations.

## Key Features

1. Landing page with hero, features, pricing, security, FAQ, testimonials, and CTA.
2. Authentication with register, login, Google login, forgot password, and two-factor authentication.
3. User dashboard showing filing progress, financial year, previous returns, refund status, tax payable, documents, and AI notifications.
4. Editable user profile with PAN, Aadhaar, DOB, residential status, address, bank accounts, employer details, nominee, and filing history.
5. Document upload for Form 16, AIS, TIS, Form 26AS, salary slips, bank statements, broker statements, investment proofs, donation receipts, and medical insurance receipts.
6. AI document extraction with confidence scores and manual correction.
7. AIS and Form 26AS import with mismatch detection.
8. Broker statement analysis and Indian capital gains computation.
9. Income detection and classification.
10. Deduction finder and tax-saving suggestions.
11. ITR recommendation with explanation.
12. AI tax assistant grounded in Indian tax rules and user documents.
13. Tax regime comparison between old and new regimes.
14. Refund estimator and validation engine.
15. ITR summary generation and exports.
16. Notifications and admin dashboard.
